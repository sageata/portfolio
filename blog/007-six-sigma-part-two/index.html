Six Sigma - Part Two

Deneb - VEGA Lite

The Power BI out-of-the-box Scatter Plot visual is very good, however it lacks a key feature that I needed to better represent crew actions such as using SVGs. Although I could probably have used Charticulator, I wanted to use Deneb as it seemed novel and more powerful.

In the picture above colors represent action types, such as serve, prepare, clear, whereas the icons represent the “what”. For example a green martini glass represents that the crew served a drink, or a yellow cheese icon shows that the crew were preparing the cheese(I hope no mice üê≠ around).

Besides the temporal axis, which do not come out of the box for such small intervals, the code was not very difficult to write, but what was more difficult was modifying the icons as VEGA Lite requires 2x2 pixel SVGs and to be perfectly centered.
